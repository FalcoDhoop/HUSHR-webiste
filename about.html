We design with nature to create high-performance, sustainable acoustic panels that elevate any space.

Our Mission

At HUSHR, we're dedicated to transforming how spaces sound and feel through sustainable innovation. We create natural acoustic solutions that respect the environment while providing exceptional performance.

By harnessing the power of mycelium – nature's own network – we offer an alternative to synthetic materials that doesn't compromise on quality or aesthetics.

Our Vision

We envision a world where buildings enhance human wellbeing through natural materials and thoughtful design. Where acoustic comfort doesn't come at the expense of our planet's health.

HUSHR aims to lead the transition to truly sustainable building materials by showing that nature already provides the solutions we need – we just need to work with it, not against it.
