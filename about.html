We design with nature to create sustainable, functional design panels that elevate any space and are built to last.

Our Mission

At HUSHR, we’re committed to reshaping how spaces sound and feel through sustainable innovation. We grow natural acoustic solutions that support a better future, by building on what yesterday left behind.

Harnessing the power of mycelium, our panels are grown using agricultural waste streams and are fully biodegradable. We offer eco-conscious solutions that never compromise on quality, creativity, or the future of our planet.

Our Vision

At HUSHR, we envision a world where the spaces we live enhance our wellbeing through natural materials and thoughtful design. A world where acoustic comfort, aesthetic value, and environmental responsibility go hand in hand.

We strive to be part of the pioneers leading the transition toward truly sustainable building practices, proving that nature already holds the answers. By working with it, not against it, we elevate spaces where innovation and ecological harmony coexist.
