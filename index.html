What are mycelium panels?

Mycelium panels are sustainable, functional design solutions made from the root structure of mushrooms. Our innovative panels offer natural sound absorption while being entirely biodegradable, combining functionality with aesthetics to bring nature's beauty inside.

Why choose HUSHR panels?

This allows us to offer an eco-conscious solution without compromising on quality, creativity, or the planet’s future.
