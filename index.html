What are Mycelium Panels?

Mycelium panels are sustainable acoustic solutions made from the root structure of mushrooms. These innovative panels offer exceptional sound absorption while being completely natural and biodegradable.

Weather-Resistant & Versatile

HUSHR panels aren’t limited to indoor use. With added treatment options, our mycelium panels can be adapted for semi-outdoor or protected outdoor environments. Their natural look complements wooden structures, green walls, and more.

Even in changing weather conditions, these panels retain their acoustic and aesthetic performance — offering a sustainable alternative wherever sound comfort is needed.

Why Choose HUSHR Panels?

Sustainable

Grown, not manufactured. Our panels are carbon-negative and fully compostable at the end of their lifecycle, leaving no waste behind.

Lightweight

Despite their excellent acoustic properties, our panels are remarkably light, making installation easy and reducing structural load.

Naturally Insulating

Beyond sound absorption, mycelium panels provide thermal insulation, helping to regulate temperature and reduce energy costs.
